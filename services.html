Services.

Life Settlement policy also commonly known as longevity assets, is nothing but a straight forward transfer of existing life insurance policy with less than its face value and more than its cash surrender value of the policy.

01

Two Fold Operation

It works in two-fold; firstly the policyholder gets the opportunity to put up their already matured policy on buy-out options and secondly, it allows the investors to invest in a matured policy and not having to go through the struggle of building it from scratch. With high profitability in a shorter span of time, this lets investors build better portfolios with better investment plans.

02

Better and Faster Results

Life Investing is basically for people who are well-above their retirement age and want to sell off their insurance policy on the market for a better return than the policy claimed estimate, but not exactly getting the same amount as they would have gotten in lieu of their demise. This is a smart way for the policyholder to get a substantially better return than the policy getting surrendered. Furthermore, it gives great investors a great portfolio building asset, by taking over a policy remaining pay-outs for a greater overall return.

03

Pick What You Like

At Lifefundo, we comprehend the hardships faced with fulfilling the monthly premium fees and thus we offer a common marketplace for the policyholder’s to either take their policy without having to pay-out the rest of the premium’s and also allows investors to pick up a diverse portfolio for better returns than the traditional options.
Lifefundo brings both the parties and offers something valuable, and true to its nature to greatly benefit both the parties.

04

A Lucrative Plan

Majorly differentiated with stocks and bonds, the Life Settlement industry is supposedly on its path to substantial growth giving a range of options to both individuals and institutional investors to enter a much stable market and overall a better chance at making a lucrative investment option plan.
